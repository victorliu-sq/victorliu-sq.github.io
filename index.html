About

I'm Jiaxin Liu, a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University (OSU). I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiaodong Zhang and Dr. Rubao Lee.

My research area focuses on algorithm engineering, with an emphasis on accelerating irregular data-intensive applications through modern hardwares.

Prior to OSU, I received my M.S. from Brown University and B.Eng. from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) .
